About A.P.J Abdul Kalam

A.P.J Abdul Kalam,the "Missile Man of India," was born on october 15,1931, in Rameshwram,Tamil Nadu. He
served as the 11 president of India and was a renoweded scientist,known for his work in India space and missile
Program.

Achivements

  • Developed India's first satellite launch vehicle(SLV-III)
  • Led India successful nuclear tests in 1998
  • Wrote books like "Wings of Fire" and "India 2020"
  • Recived the Bharat Ratan,India heigest civilian award
